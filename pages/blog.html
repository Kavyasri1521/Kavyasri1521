Excerpt from Either/Or: The 3 Mistakes of My Life

The 3 Mistakes of My Life is a captivating novel by Chetan Bhagat that follows the journey of three friends in Gujarat, India. Set against the backdrop of societal and political turmoil, the story revolves around Govind, Ish, and Omi as they navigate through love, friendship, and ambition. As they pursue their dreams of entrepreneurship, they encounter challenges that test their loyalty and integrity. Govind's love for Vidya, coupled with his aspirations for success, leads to a series of mistakes that have profound consequences. The novel delves into themes of friendship, betrayal, and redemption, highlighting the complexities of life in contemporary India. As I gazed at the flickering lights of the city below, I couldn't help but feel a sense of longing. Longing for something more, something beyond the confines of my small-town existence. But little did I know that chasing my dreams would lead to the greatest challenges of my life, and the mistakes that would change everything. This excerpt captures the protagonist's yearning for a better life and foreshadows the trials and tribulations he will face in his pursuit of success.
---Chetan Bhagat
